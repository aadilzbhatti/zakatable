How are these numbers calculated?

Detailed breakdown of the mathematical formulas and theological sources used.

Qualitative Business Screening

Rule: The company's primary business must not generate significant revenue from prohibited (haram) activities (banking, alcohol, gambling, weapons, etc.).

Tolerable Limit: Minor non-compliant revenue must not exceed 5% of total revenue (AAOIFI Standard 21, Section 3/4).

Source: Company classification profiles and audited segment disclosures.

Quantitative Financial Ratios

Threshold: Debt, Cash, and Receivables must each remain under 33% (or 33.33% under MSCI) of the valuation denominator.

Formulas:

  • Debt Ratio = Total Interest-Bearing Debt / Denominator
  • Cash Ratio = (Cash + Short-Term Investments) / Denominator
  • Receivables Ratio = Accounts Receivable / Denominator

Valuation Denominator Options: Current Market Cap, 12-Month/36-Month Average Market Cap, or Total Balance Sheet Assets.

Zakat: Balance Sheet Method

Application: Recommended for long-term/passive investors to calculate Zakat only on the net liquid zakatable wealth of the corporation (AAOIFI Standard 35, item 4/2/4).

Formula:

Net Zakatable Assets = Cash & Equivalents + Short-Term Investments + Accounts Receivable + Inventory - Current Liabilities

Per Share: Divided by the company's total shares outstanding to yield Zakatable Value Per Share.

Rates: Lunar Calendar (2.50%) or Solar Calendar (2.577% adjusted for the extra 11 days of asset accumulation).

Zakat: 30% Market Cap Proxy

Application: Backfall heuristic recommended by NZF and contemporary scholars when full balance sheet disclosures are unavailable or for fast indexing.

Formula:

Zakatable Asset Per Share = 30% × Share Price

Zakat due per share is then calculated by multiplying this proxy value by the Lunar (2.50%) or Solar (2.577%) rate.

Developer API Reference

Integrate Shariah screening and Zakat calculations directly into your software. Self-documenting, JSON-based endpoints.

Get Ticker Compliance & Zakat Ratios

GET http://localhost:8000/api/v1/screen/{ticker}

Query Parameters
Parameter Type Default Description
standard string aaoifi Screening rule standard: aaoifi, dow_jones, msci
denominator string market_cap Ratio denominator: market_cap, total_assets, trailing_market_cap_12m, trailing_market_cap_36m
refresh boolean false Bypasses server cache and pulls fresh data from Yahoo Finance
cURL Command Example
curl -X 'GET' \
  'http://localhost:8000/api/v1/screen/AAPL?standard=aaoifi&denominator=market_cap' \
  -H 'accept: application/json'
